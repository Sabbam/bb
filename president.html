The Society for Collegiate Education

Professor K.C.Reddy

President of the Society for Collegiate Education. Prof.K.C.Reddy is a accomplished academician and administrator. He is the current Chancellor of Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies Andhra Pradesh.

With Doctorate in Economics, he taught Economics at Andhra University for over three decades before joining Andhra Pradesh Government as Chairman, State Council of Higher Education in 2004. He occupied several administrative positions like Director, Academic Staff College, SAARC Studies Centre, Board of Management Member and Vice-Chancellor-in-charge at Andhra University.

He also served as Chairman Rajiv Yuvakiranalu, a flagship Skill Development and Employment Programme of united AP Government during 2011-2014 and was instrumental in skill up-gradation in providing employment to unemployed. He also served as President of the Indian Economic Association. He is the President of SIDAR (Society for Integrated Development and Research), a think tank of Academics, Administrators, and Entrepreneurs. He is a visiting Professor at Armstrong Atlantic State University, Savannah, Georgia, U.S.A.

He has been acclaimed as a Teacher and Researcher par excellence by his students and peers. He authored many books and papers. He is the recipient of many awards and honours across India and abroad. To make a special mention – President’s Award for Innovation in Higher Education from Indian Science Congress, Honorary D. Litt. from Blekinge Institute of Technology, Sweden.
